In the friendly town of Greenfield, there was a popular store owned by Mr. Davis. It was a favourite spot for the townspeople.

Emily and Jack loved helping Mr. Davis with tasks like restocking shelves, assisting customers with purchases, and handling transactions.

To handle the tasks at the store accurately and precisely, Mr. Davis thought of teaching them about decimal division making their day both educational and enjoyable.

As Emily and Jack learned decimal division, they excelled at ensuring accurate transactions and inventory management at Mr. Davis's store. Their newfound skills made them indispensable, beloved by the townspeople for their precise service. Greenfield's favourite store thrived, thanks to their dedication to precision and customer care.
